Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dormio?
Dormio is a sleep sounds, stories, and meditation app designed to help you relax and sleep better. It features curated audio scenes, nature soundscapes, sleep stories, guided meditations, and customizable sound mixing.
Is Dormio free?
Yes! All features are available without a subscription. You can optionally support development through in-app tips if you enjoy the app.
Does Dormio work offline?
Yes. Once you download scenes, you can enjoy them anytime without an internet connection.
How do I mix two sounds together?
Start playing any sound, tap the "+" button or "Add Sound" option, select a second sound to layer, then adjust individual volumes using the sliders.
What is Flow Mode?
Flow Mode creates a gentle oscillating volume effect that mimics natural breathing rhythms. This can enhance relaxation and meditation. Enable it from the player controls.
What is Loop Mode?
Loop Mode shuffles through all sounds in a category (Sleep, Relax, or Lullaby) for hours of variety without repetition.
How do I set a sleep timer?
Tap the timer icon in the player and select your desired duration (5 minutes to 8 hours). Audio will gradually fade out when the timer ends.
What is "Continue With"?
After a sleep story or guided meditation ends, Dormio can automatically continue with calming sounds of your choice—Sleep sounds, Relax sounds, or Nature sounds. You can set your preference in Settings or choose each time.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any tips or purchases made with your Apple ID.
Why does audio stop in the background?
Make sure Background App Refresh is enabled for Dormio in your device Settings. Also check that Low Power Mode is disabled and battery optimization is turned off for the app.

Troubleshooting

Audio won't play

  1. Check that your device isn't in Silent Mode
  2. Ensure volume is turned up
  3. Try closing and reopening the app
  4. Restart your device

App crashes on startup

  1. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  2. Restart your device
  3. Uninstall and reinstall the app
  4. Contact support if the issue persists

Downloads fail

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  3. Try again on WiFi if using cellular data
  4. Restart the app and retry
